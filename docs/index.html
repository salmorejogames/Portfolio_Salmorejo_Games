About Salmorejo Games

We are a videogame studio set in Spain. Our team is conformed by college students of the Videogame Desgin and Development grade. With enthusiasm and willpower we are determinated to make our name big in the industry! You can see it in our current project Tales of the Dumbgeon. We hope you can have fun with the game as much as we enjoy developing it!

Tales of the Dumbgeon is a roguelike-type action video game based on obtaining cards as equipment and abilities, which will be the main mechanic thanks to which the player will be able to reach the end of the Dumbgeon.


The Stadtnarr


The Stadtnarr have always been the village fools, the outcasts, the marginalized and the excluded of Pueblo Boñiga. So any situation they can get rid of is good and the Dumbgeon is the best option.


The Stadtnarr, since saying its full name would be impossible, is the player-controlled character. Every time he dies, a new member of the Stadtnarr family is sent to the dungeon to defeat Jojomamalo.


Wizard Jojomamalo


A long time ago, in a very distant dimension, a young magician named Jojomamalo discovered the power of the Cards, mysterious artifacts that regulate everything that exists in the universe: the stars, the elements, the living beings, the weekends ...


With this power the magician was able to create at will as if he were a God, but something was holding him back, his lack of originality.


For this reason, he devoted all his efforts to creating a card that would allow him to move through other universes in what is known as The Mazoverse.


Main Character: The Stadtnarr

The Stadtnarr have always been the village fools, the outcasts, the marginalized and the excluded of Pueblo Boñiga. So any situation they can get rid of is good and the Dumbgeon is the best option.

The Cards

Created by Jojomamalo, these items have the power to alter the universe itself and they will be your best weapon to reach the end of the Dumbgeon.
